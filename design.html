Z Patch
Creativity
Prompt: Redesign the sleep experience Solution: College Students need a better way of managing their time and turning socially or educationally unproductive time into sleep. Our solution monitors brain waves to diagnose when productivity is dropping and nudge the user towards sleep instead of procrastination.
Scholastic Book Fair
Play
Prompt: Redesign the Scholastic Book Fair Solution: Incorporate a book drive into the book fair event so that underprivaledged students have an opportunity to purchase discounted books. Also, add a voucher system so that teachers can offset need by rewarding high-performing or economically-struggling students with tickets to be used to purchase books at the fair.
The Presidential Games
Play
Prompt: Redesign the voting experience Solution: Citizens miss the opportunity to vote because they are uneducated and unwilling to make the effort to become educated. Our solution is like a Hunger Games or American Idol for voting where candidates compete on a number of tasks testing leadership skills, intelligence, and other skills desirable in a president. The public is engaged by the tv-show, thereby becoming educated and creating a social pressure to engage in voting.
ShoeTunes
Play
Prompt: How might people have more fun in their Vans?
Solution: Introverts need a better way to connect with themselves and the people around them through physical play. Our ShoeTune accessory lets the wearer sync their music to their movements, or have our iPhone app machine learn common sound effects for repetitive motions. Our accessory is equipped with a gyroscope and accelerometer and can be used by an individual to inspire personal introspection or by a group to create a creative song through physical play, thereby connecting the group.
