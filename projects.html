Synconnect: A Virtual Ambient Display for Synchronous Online Collaboration
Research Topics in HCI
My friend Rio Akasaka and I developed and studied a tool for encouraging synchronous online collaboration. We wanted to visualize when an individual was actively working on a collaborative task, and communicate that to their partner(s). We hypothesized that individuals would collaborate better when they could interact in real time, even if not in person. We noticed during our study that once we made the time contribution visible, the two partners put in almost exactly the same time commitment to the project. We wrote a paper which we submitted to the CSCW conference and got great feedback.
